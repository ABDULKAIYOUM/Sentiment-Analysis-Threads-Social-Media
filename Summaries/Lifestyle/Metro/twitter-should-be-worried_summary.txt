A wholesale redesign to provide something materially different to Twitter would make little commercial sense, and as advertised, it is essentially Instagram repurposed to focus on text before images.   Many have tried to steal Twitter’s crown as king of the social media jungle, but most were quickly lost, caught in the vines and fighting for any scraps the little blue bird dropped while soaring above the trees. But Threads has been helicoptered in, delivered with the backing of tech behemoth Meta and a ready-made audience of literally billions courtesy of its sister app Instagram.
