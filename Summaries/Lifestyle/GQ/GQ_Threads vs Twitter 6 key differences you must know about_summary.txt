You need an Instagram account to use Threads While you do not need to be a user of any other app to use Twitter, you need to have an Instagram account to use Threads. You cannot view the list of likes On Twitter, you can view the names of the people who have liked a particular tweet, But on Threads, you cannot view other people's likes.   Threads allow users to share texts and images, engage in real-time conversations, and directly compete with Twitter.   However, on Twitter, users without the verified blue badge can post videos with a maximum duration of two minutes and 20 seconds.
