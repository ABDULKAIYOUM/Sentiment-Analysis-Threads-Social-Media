Instagram’s Twitter alternative: What we know so far  In her ICYMI newsletter, Ms Haberman says Instagram’s Twitter-like app will be here by the end of June. For all of the aforementioned strengths and opportunities of Meta, the new Instagram app for text-based conversations is a threat to Twitter. The UI of the app consists of text posts and threads like Twitter but the heart, comment bubble, and paper plane-shaped share icons are comparable to Instagram. If Meta’s app turns out to be even decent, it could attract Twitter users and many of these users are likely to already have an Instagram account. Meta considers this app as a way for creators, public figures, and probably regular users of Instagram to share their interests.
